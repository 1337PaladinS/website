The Problem

How can Chinese children ensure the safety and happiness of up to 6 elderly relatives (parents, grand-parents), while being in full time employment?

Who We Help

Mudan is a Community centre providing a variety of social services to the local communities, mainly elderly people. We help people facing a wide range of social issues. We welcome people from all backgrounds and operates an open-door policy.

Who We Are

Mudan, a registered charity formed to bring together all the work undertaken across the country. This organisation is dedicated to helping elderly as well as young people and families in addition to the vulnerable adults served by Mudan.

Our Aim

Our aim, is to provide a service that enhances the quality of life of our clients and bring together people from all backgrounds helping build a better community by giving working youngsters more freedom and at the same time providing an enjoyable, safe environment for their elderly dependents. We do this by providing activities to help elderly people feel they still matter and engage more with each other, and learn something new while having fun. We run activities and skills sessions daily and offer everyone a healthy breakfast.
