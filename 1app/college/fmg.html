Overview

FMG Group of Institutions, a non-profit educational enterprise was established in the year 2010. The group was promoted by M/S Fashion Makers Group having an international standing for more than 3 decades. FMG Institute is one of the leading and reputed education institute based at Greater Noida which provides Bachelor in fine art (BFA) and Bachelor of Fashion and Apparel Design (BFAD) degrees. The groups started its first academic program, Master in Business Administration (MBA) in the department of management and technology. The program was approved by AICTE, Govt. of India and got affiliation from AKTU formally known as UPTU, Lucknow UP.
