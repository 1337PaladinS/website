How to Apply

Initiate your admission process to the college of your choice!

Hi there, Welcome to the 1Application webservice,
Mission: To get you great institutions and programs


It is a simple process starting with your registration and ending with your succsessful college application.

Let's get you started on your higher education journey.

Initiation

  1. Please log on to 1Application.
  2. After exploring the website, please sign up using this link.
  3. You can also see this link by clicking on the top right menu tab shown.

Registration

  1. Fill out the form with the details requested.
  2. You should get a message saying registration successful.
  3. Please login using your username/password here.

Dashboard View

  1. You will land on the student dashboard.
  2. This page is for reviewing institutes/courses and applying.
  3. We will visit this in the end.

1Application Form

  1. Open 1Application tab from left pane.
  2. Click blue edit button on top right to start editing your app.
  3. Click blue save button on top right to save changes.

1Application Form - Dialog Boxes

  1. Many sections like address and sibling details have dialog boxes.
  2. Fill these sections after clicking edit and before clicking save.
  3. Click add after entering and complete filling the rest of the page.

1Application Form - Document Upload

  1. This is a set of documents pertinent for your admission.
  2. Click browse and select the relevant docs.
  3. Please give it upto 60 secs to upload, the screen will reset if successful.

Advanced search Dashboard

  1. You can search via the criteria shown. (Eg. displayed via University)
  2. Please select the checkbox for institutes you want to apply to.
  3. Click the blue plus icon on the bottom left to add it to the dashboard.

Student Application Dashboard

  1. You will find the added institutes on the dashboard page.
  2. Tick the institutes you want to apply to.
  3. Select your course of choice from drop down.

Application Process - 1

  1. Selections made, click the blue apply button on the bottom right.
  2. You can review the charges here, decimal value for round off.
  3. Click pay to continue process.

Application Process - 2

  1. Fill in your contact details (email, phone number).
  2. Ensure your details are current and valid.
  3. Select your payment mode of choice.

Application Process - 3

  1. This example uses a dummy visa card as payment mode.
  2. Fill in the accurate details of your card.
  3. Click pay.

Application Process - 4

  1. Follow the sample authentication steps of your finance provider.
  2. You will get the message like the one shown, save the challan number.
  3. Even if you fail to do so, it will be mailed to you or contact us here.

Verification

  1. After a brief refresh the course and college will show up.
  2. The processed institution table will show similarly.
  3. You may keep editing your form, or update your documents, when the institute considers your form for admission, the latest application and docs will be given.

Regulations

1Application aims to give competent services based on our bound terms of use and contractual admission licensing.

As a ground rule, we respect your need for privacy and do not share your consumer data with any third party.
